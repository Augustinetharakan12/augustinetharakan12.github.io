About 250 Social

A neighbourhood table, with a Pacific point of view.

We opened our doors with one idea: bring people together over honest food and good drinks. From the first morning coffee to the last late-night cocktail, every plate that leaves our kitchen is built around what's local, fresh, and in season.

Stop in for a quick bite at the bar, settle in for dinner with friends, or grab the patio on a sunny afternoon. However you join us — you're welcome here.
